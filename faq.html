    Are You Official?

    No


    Who are you & why are you doing this?

    We are just 2 guys from Goa & the reason why we are doing this is because currently many fake rumours are spreading about COVID-19 & we wanted to come up with something that would help people of Goa with all the necessary information providing under one website.


    How often you update?

    The India's stats are updated as and when MoHFW updates them & the Goa's stats are updated as and when new cases are identified or after every 12 hours.


    How is the data gathered for Goa?

    We collect the details from state press releases & official government links & then data is validated and published into an API.
